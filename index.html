Teaching the youth the basics of basketball and helping them find their love and passion for the game

This Program is open to boys and girls ages 8 – 12.

Thrisha Boopathi

My Journey So Far…

My name is Thrisha Boopathi, I am 14 years old and I have been playing basketball for 7 years now. I started my journey by playing in a rec program called Master Sports in second grade, this was a good program to ease my way into playing basketball. Third grade was when I realized that I could become a good player. I joined a program called Bulldogs and this program was filled with many experienced coaches that taught me the basics of this sport. What was great about this program was that not only did they teach me the proper way to dribble, shoot, pass, and play defense. They also taught me good sportsmanship, the ability to be coachable, and also the importance of being able to connect and communicate with my teammates. I wish to teach the same with this program! After 4 years of being a part of the Bulldogs program, I decided to move to another program called She’s Empowered Sports (S.E.S). I made this decision because the head girls coach of Bulldogs (Courtney Clements, former WNBA player) decided to create her program (S.E.S) most of my teammates moved to her program and I moved along with them. After almost a year of playing with S.E.S, playing with club teams came to an end. It is now the summer before I start high school and I have already started to practice and play games with my high-school team at Canyon Crest Academy. With whatever experience I have gained in the past 7 years my goal is to pass on as much knowledge as I can to all who decide to participate in this program! Thank you!

Trainings will be held every Thursday from 6-7:00 pm at the address below

13030 Ashley Falls Dr, San Diego, CA 92130

What do I wear?

Players are encouraged to wear short sleeved T-shirts and shorts and a pair of outdoor basketball shoes or running shoes. Also please do not forget to bring water. 🙂

What to expect…

These clinics will include the basic principals of basketball such as: ball handling, form shooting, defensive techniques, communication skills, and building a stronger IQ for the game. I will focus on making everyone feel welcome and enjoy the process of progress.
